Frequently Asked Questions

What are the stadium regulations?

Our stadium regulations include bag size restrictions, prohibited items list, and code of conduct. Please visit our stadium guide page for detailed information.

How can I join the Eagles FC fan club?

You can join our fan club by registering on our website or visiting the club office. Membership includes exclusive benefits and special events.

Are stadium tours available?

Yes, we offer guided stadium tours on non-match days. Tours can be booked online or at the stadium visitor center. Group bookings are also available.
